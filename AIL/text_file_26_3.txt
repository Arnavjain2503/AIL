Agra is world-renowned for the Taj Mahal, a symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. The Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri are significant Mughal-era monuments. The craftsmanship of marble artisans in Agra is impressive. Local markets are filled with souvenirs and handicrafts. Agra's history is deeply rooted in Mughal architecture and culture. Yamuna River adds scenic beauty to the city. Agra is an essential stop in India's Golden Triangle circuit.
