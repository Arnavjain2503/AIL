Kerala, God's own country, offers backwaters, beaches, and wildlife sanctuaries. The houseboat rides in Alleppey are mesmerizing. The tea plantations of Munnar provide breathtaking views. The Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is home to elephants and tigers. Kerala's Kathakali dance and Ayurvedic therapies are world-famous. The cuisine, rich in coconut and spices, is another highlight. Kumarakom and Wayanad are also must-visit destinations. Kerala is an amazing blend of nature, culture, and tradition.
